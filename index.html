About Us

The Information and Communications University (ICU) is registered with the Higher Education Authority (HEA), under the Ministry of Higher Education to offer distance (e-Learning), and full-time education in Zambia.

ICU collaborates with National Research University "Moscow Power Engineering Institute", Moscow, Russia, in the field of scientific research, teaching innovation, staff and student exchanges, and joint education programs. ICU also partners with Volgograd Pedagogical University (VSSPU) for academic and cultural exchanges.
